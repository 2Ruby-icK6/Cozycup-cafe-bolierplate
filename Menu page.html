Our Coffee Menu

AMERICANO $2.50 - $4.50

Made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water,
resulting in a coffee that's similar in strength to
drip coffee but with an espresso flavor.

TEA $3 - $5

Steeped leaves in hot water, a classic and comforting beverage.

CAPPUCINO $3 - $5

Made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk,
and milk foam. It's often garnished with a dusting
of cocoa powder or cinnamon.

BOBBA MILKTEA $3 - $5

Sweet tea with milk and chewy tapioca pearls.

ESPRESSO $2 - $4

A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing a small amount
of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans.
It forms the base for many other coffee beverages.

LATTE $3 - $5

Similar to a cappuccino but with more steamed milk and less
foam. It's often flavored with syrups like vanilla or caramel.

ICED COFFEE $3.50 - $5.50

Cold brewed coffee served over ice, a refreshing pick-me-up.

Our Pastries Menu

CINNAMON ROLLS $2.50 - $4.50

These sweet, sticky rolls are made from a yeasted dough,
swirled with cinnamon and sugar, and topped with
icing or glaze. They're a delightful indulgence
alongside a cup of coffee.

CROISSANT $2 - $4

A classic French pastry known for its flaky and buttery layers.

COFFEE CAKE $3 - $5

Coffee cake is a moist, crumbly cake often flavored
with cinnamon and topped with a streusel or crumb topping.
It's designed to complement coffee perfectly.

PANCAKE $3 - $5

Fluffy, round breakfast cake with syrup.

SCONES $2 - $4

Scones are a British pastry that can be sweet
or savory. Common sweet flavors include cranberry-orange,
lemon-poppyseed, and cinnamon. They're often served with
clotted cream and jam.

DANISH PASTRIES $3 - $5

Danish pastries come in a variety of shapes and fillings,
including fruit, cream cheese, almond, and cinnamon.
They're typically sweet, flaky, and delicious.

MIXED BERRY TART $3.50 - $5.50

Berry-filled pastry, sweet and glazed.
